Protection and Privacy Policy

We will only use personal information we collect in accordance with the laws of the United States. We will therefore aim to ensure that all employees, contractors, agents, suppliers or consultants of Matthew621.net who have access to any personal data held by or on behalf of Matthew621.net, are fully aware of and abide by their legal duties and responsibilities.

Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding personal data that we collect from you, or that you provide to us, in connection with your use of our website at www.Matthew621.net ("Site") and the services provided by Scott Porter ("Services"). By using the Site you are accepting and consenting to the use of your data as described in this policy.

Changes to this Data Protection and Privacy Policy
We may revise, modify or update this Policy. Please check this policy frequently for any changes as your continued use of the Site or Services indicates your consent to such changes.

What is Personal Data?

Personal data is any information about a specific or definable natural person which may be used to identify them. Personal data includes information such as an individual's name, home and work addresses, email address, phone number and financial information.

Protection Objectives

In order to provide the services, Matthew621.net collects and uses personal data about people. This includes the personal data of members of the public, employees, clients and customers, suppliers and partners.

Third Party Websites

There may be links contained in the Site or Services which redirect you to other websites. For example, when you access the services of a third party such as a Church Management System to import your data. This Policy does not apply to such third party websites which we do not operate, and we do not accept any responsibility or liability for these third party sites. If you have concerns about your privacy when using such services, please review the privacy policies of those websites which are not connected to us.

Information We Collect and When We Collect It

Following is the type of information we collect and when we collect the information we require:

Upon registration with us.

Through your use of our products or the Services.
When an inquiry is made with us and through communication with you.
Automatically received and recorded on our server logs from your browser, when visiting our Site, including your login information, browser type and version, operating system and platform, IP address, cookies, and the pages you request.

We process this data because it is necessary to fulfil our contractual obligations to you to provide the Services. We regard data provided to us as confidential and only share it with consent or if we are legally required to do so and in accordance with this Policy.

  • To monitor patterns of usage, such as login dates and volumes of data, so we can understand how people are using the Site and the Services.
  • For security reasons, identification and authentication.
  • We may obtain your IP address when you use the Site. We use your IP address, browser and session information for statistical and analytical purposes and to monitor and prevent fraud and abuse.

Notwithstanding the above, visitors to our Site may browse it anonymously and no personal data will be collected unless provided to us.

Information we receive from other sources

We work closely with third parties (including, for example, business partners; subcontractors in technical, payment and delivery services; advertising networks; analytics providers; search information providers; credit reference agencies) and may receive information about you from them.

Use of Cookies

Like many other web services, we use a standard technology called "cookies." Some cookies are essential to make websites work properly. Others help provide a better user experience. Our cookies do not gather any personal data, and any information gathered by the use of cookies is compiled on an aggregate and anonymous basis. We use cookies to offer a better service for our users. If you visit our Site and reject all cookies, you may not be able to take full advantage of all the features of our Site and Services. For more information about cookies, please visit www.cookiecentral.com or www.aboutcookies.org.

Data Transfer

In order to provide the Services you request, it may be necessary for your data to be transferred to and stored by mobile network operators, telecommunications providers or other organisations in countries outside America ("the US"). Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access.

Security for all personally-identifiable information (including all cell phone numbers, texts, messages and other data) is our priority. Once we receive your information, we take reasonable measures to secure it on our systems and do so in accordance with our Security Policy.
